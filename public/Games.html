Locomotive 115

Not every train that sets out on the Widow's Run is fortunate enough to return. Few conductors have braved this cold expanse and survived, fewer still have braved it and kept their wits intact to tell the tale. Desperate accounts told by men driven to the edge; stories of perilous storms and deadly colds where the heat of your furnace is your only lifeline... and myths of strange creatures that lurk in the darkness. Fight your way to escape the rails and outrun the storm in this single-player, survival horror game.
